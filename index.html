About

Professional Information

9+ years of academic teaching, training, and consultancy experience

Dr. Jitendra Kumar Verma is an Assistant Professor of Information Technology & Knowledge Management at the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT), where he works at the intersection of technology, analytics, and management. His work focuses on bridging academia and industry through applied analytics and technology strategy, with an emphasis on enabling data-driven decision-making in business environments.

He holds B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering. His academic and research interests span digital transformation, business analytics, enterprise information systems, process automation, artificial intelligence and machine learning, cloud computing, Internet of Things (IoT), blockchain, FinTech, and emerging technologies for managerial applications.

Dr. Verma is a recipient of the prestigious DAAD Fellowship and has served as a Visiting Scholar at Julius Maximilians University of Würzburg, Germany. He is also UGC NET-JRF qualified and GATE qualified in Computer Science, and holds the distinction of being a Senior Member of IEEE.

His academic and professional credentials include Master's and Doctorate degrees from JNU, a Visiting Scholar association with Julius Maximilians University of Würzburg, Germany, recognition as an IICA Approved Independent Director, and FDP alumnus status from IIM Ahmedabad.

At IIFT, he actively contributes to institutional leadership through his responsibilities in the Corporate and Placement Division. His teaching portfolio covers a wide range of technology and analytics-oriented courses, including Management Information Systems, Database Management Systems, Data Structures using Python, IT Applications in Management, Data and Database Management Systems, and Technology Trends in Business. His teaching approach emphasizes practical application, industry relevance, and analytical thinking.

Prior to joining IIFT, Dr. Verma has been associated with Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College, University of Delhi, Galgotias University, and Amity University Haryana. Beyond academia, he is actively involved in executive education, industry-oriented training programmes, and consultancy engagements in the domains of business analytics, digital technologies, and technology-enabled management practices.

His broader objective is to contribute to the evolving landscape of technology-driven business by integrating academic research, industry collaboration, and practical implementation frameworks.
